About Kyara

At Kyara Beverages

We reimagine refreshment by crafting millet-based fruit drinks that not only quench thirst but also nourish the body. Rooted in India's rich agricultural heritage and powered by modern innovation, our mission is to bring wholesome, delicious, and sustainable beverages to every household.
